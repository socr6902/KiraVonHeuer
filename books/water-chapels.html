Water Chapels

Exhibition Documentation

Hardcover, 156 pages
24cm × 30cm
Published 2024
Photography by Aurélien Mole

Complete documentation of the Water Chapels exhibition at Galerie Marian Goodman, Paris. Installation photography, architectural drawings, and process notes alongside essays by sound artist Cathy Lane and writer Marina Warner.

Printed on uncoated Munken paper with exposed binding. The book's physicality mirrors the exhibition's attention to materials and their qualities—weight, texture, the way light moves across surfaces.

Essays

  • "Listening to Water" by Cathy Lane
  • "Threshold Spaces and Sacred Doubt" by Marina Warner
  • "Making Water Chapels" by Kira von Heuer
