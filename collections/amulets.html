Amulets

Small objects made to carry. Protectors, anchors, reminders. Cast in sterling silver from wax models shaped by hand, each carries marks of the making—imperfections that make them particular.

These are not jewelry in the decorative sense. They are companions. Objects to hold in moments of doubt or transition. To wear close. To keep in pockets. To gift with intention.
